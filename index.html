Join Today

You can get more engaged with NCHS through 3 easy steps:

1. Sign Up

Click on the "Get Started" button up to sign up today to create an account! You can use the profile button to track your points.

2. Discover Opportunities

Use the activities tab to sign up for upcoming events! You can see the point value for each event, and the price of it's ticket. Redeem your points for rewards! You can get free sweatshirts, lunch item foods, and free tickets to school events!

3. Stay Informed

Use the "Newsletter" tab to learn about happenings at NCHS. Also, stay on the lookout for our user of the month!
