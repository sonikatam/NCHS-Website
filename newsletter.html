FBLA's Biggest Victory

Over this past summer, Our FBLA chapter went to Chicago, Illinois to attend FBLA’s National Leadership Conference! During this time, the members got to explore the windy city with their friends and compete in competitions. This year was their most successful year thus far! Let’s take a look at their accomplishments:
46 national qualifiers in 34 competitive events! This is a fantastic accomplishment to compete alongside the best in the country!

On the big stage - 12 of their members in 7 events got a trophy! Another NCHS record!

1st Place in Public Speaking - Avani Rai (our first Individual National Champ)
3rd Place in Life Smarts - Ved Kommalapati and Yatin Gupta
7th Place in American Enterprise Project - Sonika Tamilarisan, Amirtha Vijay, and Sumukh Sayani
8th Place in Business Financial Plan - Rishi Bandhari, Lalit Kudikala, and Akhil Kanneganti
9th Place in Organizational Leadership - Nina Osborne
10th Place in Intro to Financial Math - Akshay Pespunuri
10th Place in Mobile App Development - Shreni Jain

Fantastic accomplishment by our chapter, and we can't wait to see what new records they will set this year!

If you want to attend this year's FBLA National Leadership Conference, join FBLA! This year their chapter is going to Atlanta, Georgia!

Contact Mrs. Pendleton for more information!
